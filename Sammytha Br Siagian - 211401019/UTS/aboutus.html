Love Avoskin, Love Earth

Avoskin's commitment to nature conservation and care for the earth is manifested in every product. Avoskin only uses natural ingredients that are not harmful to the environment and certainly provide benefits for the skin. Besides, Avoskin uses glass packaging that can be recycled more easily. This reduces plastic waste and other rubbish.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Avoskin is committed to continuing to make improvements from the composition side to our commitment to support the green beauty concept. In 2020, Avoskin is committed to reformulating and presenting several products with a clean beauty concept. This clean beauty concept covers the selection of product raw materials to the packaging used. Avoskin is more focused on paying attention to the quality of its products through ingredients that are clean and more eco-friendly. In terms of packaging, Avoskin uses eco-friendly plastic with sustainable materials from sugar cane, one of them.

In terms of composition, Avoskin is committed to presenting products with a minimum of ingredients that are alcohol free, SLS free, paraben free, fragrance free, silicone free and no animal testing. The natural extracts in Avoskin products also come from various parts of the world and are picked manually by local farmers in the morning to maintain their quality.
