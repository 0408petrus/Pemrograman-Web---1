Salvation™ is a turn-based tactical RPG/adventure game that takes place in an expansive open world on the brink of destruction interweaved with a deeply touching and personal journey about acceptance and finding meaning in death.


ABOUT THE GAME

DETERMINE YOUR OWN PATH

You are let free to explore the world from the very start. The skills, equipment and means of transportations you choose will determine the paths that are open to you, making every choice matter. Every player will be able to chart their own unique path and experience their own set of adventures through the world of Disdear in their journey to awaken all 13 shrines.

PLAY YOUR WAY

Combat is an integral part of Salvation™ gameplay. Your party can choose to learn a set of unique skills from a skill tree in addition to equipping themselves with items and weapons with their own set of stats and abilities, allowing each player to tailor their party to their own unique playstyle.

AN INTIMATE STORY

The story of Salvation™ takes place in a dying world, besieged by countless monsters. Each person your party meets in their adventures has their own stories to tell and their own perspective. But even though set in a large world with its own complexities, the main story is a personal one, about two people meeting in unlikely circumstances and growing together by learning about each other.
