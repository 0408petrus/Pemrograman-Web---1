PREMISE
The world, Eshla, is in ruins. A broken promise left the Jhins scattered and ravenous. Great kingdoms and empires are now small cities cowering behind stone walls and moats. But the prophecy foretold of a maiden, Edun-ro, who will mend the world by resurrecting all thirteen shrines. Luther, a man with a debt, is tasked by the Order to escort the maiden across her pilgrimage and to return before the Eclipse will sink the world into darkness.

WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of Indonesian developers based in Medan, North Sumatera seeking to create immersive and once-in-a-lifetime experiences. We believe in the ability to create personalized stories through gameplay while telling a coherent narrative.
Our goal is simple, tell relatable, heartfelt and personal narratives accompanied with a fun gameplay system and we invite anyone who shares this vision to join us.
