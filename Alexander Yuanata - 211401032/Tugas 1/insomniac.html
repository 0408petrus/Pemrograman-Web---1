A TOWN SHROUDED IN MYSTERY

Twenty four year old Mark Ashwyn should've had nothing connecting him and the rural town of Paulo's Bay, but despite the reservations of his loved ones, he went anyways. As a victim of a recent insomnia epidemic, Mark was promised by a community of victims that they were cured of their afflictions by a shaman who lives in the town.
But when he arrives, he finds the town covered in fog with no signs of life and is attacked by something. He wakes up with none of his luggage inside a run-down apartment, the windows are sealed with wooden boards and talismans and screeches could be heard from outside. What has happened to this town?

BEYOND FEAR

As Mark roams the town he will encounter hideous creatures, an unholy concoction of flesh and machine seeking to devour all living beings. Fight them with an arsenal of crude weapons but beware, supplies are scarce but monsters are aplenty.

SCAVENGE AND SURVIVE

Scavenge the town for medkits, ammunition and weapons to defend yourself against the horde of undying monsters. Fighting might provide momentary respite but avoiding confrontation preserves supplies that you desperately need.

GENERAL INFORMATION

Genre : Survival Horror
