A HOPELESS WORLD

After a solar flare demolished most technological innovations and blanketed the sky in ash, humanity has slowly died off. A small community deep in the Indian subcontinent lives day-to-day in a pre-war bunker, hunting animals to sustain themselves. During a small expedition, the Sigma Echelon finds a cryogenic chamber storing a young woman with the knowledge to change the world.

JOURNEY FOR TWO

Codename Hecate, the leader of Sigma Echelon. A human genetically modified to withstand the harsh temperatures of post-apocalyptic Earth seeking to find meaning in a meaningless life.
Alcina, a pathfinder of the Peacekeeper, an old world organization created to preserve humanity during apocalyptic events. Frozen for decades until awakened by the Sigma Echelon.
Parallel paths that by chance crossed briefly, these two set off on a journey through Asia and Australia towards Antarctica in order to activate a failsafe that will save the world.

DELVE INTO DEPRAVED MINDS

During their journey, Hecate and Alcina will cross paths with survivors, broken and battered by the apocalypse. Explore a world without law and order, where morality is only a suggestion. Will the journey change these two for better or for worse?

SAVE THE WORLD

Project Elysium. A space station floating above the South Pole, accessible by a space elevator on Antarctica. Elysium holds a device that will 'fix' the climate, effectively saving the last remnants of humanity, or so Alcina says.
