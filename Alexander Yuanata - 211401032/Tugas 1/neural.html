WAR NEVER CHANGES

It's the year 2113. Earth has been decimated by decades of bombardment, all semblances of nations are gone, leaving behind many city-state hiding behind pockets of safety. In the recent years, a city-state called 'The Empire' has started numerous wars with surrounding city-states, dominating in infrantry-based combat due to their usage of 'Neural Engines', a central human brain controlling numerous robotic soldiers. You are a DX-113, a Neural Engine stationed on the Eastern Front of former Europe who has gained sentience and is plotting an escape.

FIGHT FOR THE FUTURE

You are a one man army. Align yourself with one of many city-states that have banded together in a war against the Empire. Accomplish various objectives to raise the respective faction's power with the final power structure determining the future of Earth, if there's any future at all.

ASSEMBLE A TEAM

Hijack into the Empire's network of robots and recruit them into your squad or recruit mercenaries from any of the city-states that you have curried favor with. Robots are emotionless and can perfectly execute orders but humans may take additional actions, gain buffs and protect each other while morale is high.

ANALYZE AND EXECUTE

Scan the battlefield with a bird's eye view. Equip your squad with appropriate equipment for specific missions. Plan your moves carefully and pray that the enemies miss.

GENERAL INFORMATION

Genre : Tactical Turn-based Strategy
Age rating : 16+
